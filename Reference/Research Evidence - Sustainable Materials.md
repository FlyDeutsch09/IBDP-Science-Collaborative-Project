# Research Evidence: Sustainable Materials for Acoustic Metamaterials

> Source: Web research conducted June 2026.

## 1. Recycled Plastic Metamaterials

### Recycled PET Bottles (Review, 2026, *Sustainable Materials and Technologies*)
- 80 peer-reviewed studies analyzed
- rPET engineered into resonant, porous, hybrid, and multilayer structures
- Effective low-frequency absorption and insulation
- High circular economy potential: addresses both plastic waste AND noise pollution
- Challenges: scalability, waste feedstock variability, fire safety, testing standardization
- Current TRL: 3–4 (moving toward industry-ready)

### Upcycled PVC Honeycomb (2026, *Sustainability*)
- Three-layer PVC diaphragm + honeycomb core + air gaps
- Strong absorption in 250–600 Hz band
- Portable impedance tube measurements validated
- Explicit building/architecture application focus
- Post-industrial PVC waste reuse

## 2. Bio-Based & Natural Materials

### Cork Membrane + Honeycomb (Aug 2025, *Buildings*)
- Cork membranes coupled with aramid paper honeycomb cores
- Effective at low (250–500 Hz) and mid (500–1400 Hz) frequencies
- Modular, fully recyclable, low-cost
- Multiple membranes broaden bandwidth
- Minimal thickness, low environmental impact

### Seaweed (Ulva) Foam (UW, 2025–2026)
- Green seaweed freeze-cast into acoustic foams
- **Outperformed commercial polyester panels** in 0–2000 Hz band
- Near-unity absorption at ~1.4 kHz
- First quantitative LCA for algae-based acoustic insulation
- Processing energy identified as primary carbon hotspot

### Plasma-Treated Bamboo (2025, *Composites Part B*)
- Tree frog-inspired hierarchical topography
- Record SAC of 0.82±0.03 at 2000–4000 Hz
- Transmission loss >55 dB at 1000–6300 Hz (6mm, 0.5 g/cm³)
- 2.6× better than polypropylene (29.6 dB vs 11.6 dB)
- Self-healing functionality (34% scratch recovery)

## 3. Commercial Products & Near-Market

### Lios SoundBounce® (EU TANDEM Project, 2025–2026)
- Active thixotropic material in cellular housing
- 4× smaller volume than traditional materials
- Safe, recoverable, recyclable at end-of-life
- Validated with European Space Agency
- Target: construction, automotive, aerospace, power generation

### Metacoustic Metablocker (France, 2026)
- Patented composite metamaterial
- Half the weight of traditional (5 kg/m² vs 10 kg/m²)
- Reduced carbon footprint
- Tested with Naval Group, SNCF, Chantiers de l'Atlantique
- Commercial rollout planned 2026

## 4. Sustainable Design Principles (from research)

1. **Dual problem-solving**: noise pollution (€100B/yr in Europe) + plastic waste (16.1M tonnes EU packaging, 2022)
2. **Life-cycle thinking**: LCA and LCC integrated into material selection
3. **Circularity**: Design for disassembly, recyclability, and bio-degradation
4. **Bio-inspiration**: Tree frog skin, cochlea, cuttlebone as performance templates
5. **Local materials**: Natural fibers from regional agricultural waste (bagasse, coir, bamboo)
